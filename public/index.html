About

STI College Marikina’s SHS EXPO 2025 stands as a premier event celebrating the creativity and innovation of its Grade 12 students. This year’s highlight is 'Sentro de Museo', an Art Exhibit featuring a vibrant collection of artworks and sculptures inspired by local Filipino festivals. Blending traditional cultural elements with modern artistic approaches, the students showcase not only their deep appreciation for Filipino heritage but also their ability to reimagine it through their original perspectives.
