Parola

TOPER-201A

The Parola Sculpture stands as a towering symbol of guidance and resilience, inspired by the iconic lighthouses that have safeguarded sailors through tumultuous seas. Crafted with a modern yet timeless aesthetic, it blends the sturdy form of a traditional lighthouse with contemporary geometric elements, signifying both steadfastness and progress.
